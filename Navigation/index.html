Keeping cooking simple

Minimalist foods are simple, whole, minimally processed foods. Minimalist food is made into simple meals with few ingredients and steps. Think: sheet pan meals or one pot meals with 10 ingredients or less. The majority of a minimalist's diet is composed of core foods that are minimally processed and minimally packaged.
